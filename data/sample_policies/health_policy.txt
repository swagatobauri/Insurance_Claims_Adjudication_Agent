PREMIUM HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY
Policyholder: Jane Smith
Policy Number: HE-123456789
Effective Date: March 1, 2024 to February 28, 2025

I. COVERAGE DETAILS
1. Inpatient Hospital Services: Covers room and board, general nursing care, and other hospital services and supplies provided while the insured is admitted as an inpatient.
2. Outpatient Medical Services: Includes primary care physician visits, specialist consultations, diagnostic tests, x-rays, and laboratory procedures.
3. Prescription Drugs: Provides coverage for generic, preferred brand, and non-preferred brand prescription medications when purchased at in-network pharmacies.
4. Emergency Services: Covers emergency room visits, ambulance transportation, and emergency care for severe, life-threatening injuries or illnesses.
5. Preventive Care: Includes annual physical exams, routine immunizations, mammograms, colonoscopies, and other preventive screenings at no cost when provided by an in-network provider.
6. Mental Health and Substance Abuse: Covers outpatient therapy, counseling, and inpatient psychiatric care or substance abuse treatment programs.

II. EXCLUSIONS
This policy does not cover the following:
1. Cosmetic Surgery: Any surgical procedures performed solely to improve appearance, unless deemed medically necessary for reconstructive purposes following an injury or illness.
2. Experimental Treatments: Any drugs, devices, or procedures classified as experimental, investigational, or unproven by standard medical boards.
3. Long-Term Care: Custodial care, long-term nursing home care, or assisted living facilities.
4. Alternative Medicine: Treatments such as acupuncture, chiropractic care (beyond initial evaluation), homeopathy, and massage therapy.
5. Elective Procedures: Any medical care or treatment that is not medically necessary.

III. CLAIM LIMITS
1. Annual Maximum Benefit: This policy does not have an annual maximum benefit for essential health benefits.
2. Lifetime Maximum Benefit: No lifetime limit for covered essential health services.
3. Out-of-Pocket Maximum: The maximum out-of-pocket limit for the policy period is $6,500 for an individual and $13,000 for a family. Once this limit is reached, the plan pays 100% of covered medical expenses for the remainder of the year.

IV. DEDUCTIBLE INFORMATION
1. Annual Deductible: The individual deductible is $1,500 per year, and the family deductible is $3,000 per year.
2. Co-payments: A $30 co-payment is required for primary care visits, and a $50 co-payment applies for specialist visits.
3. Co-insurance: After the deductible is met, the plan covers 80% of in-network services, leaving the insured responsible for 20% co-insurance up to the out-of-pocket maximum.
