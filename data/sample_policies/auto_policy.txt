COMPREHENSIVE AUTO INSURANCE POLICY
Policyholder: John Doe
Policy Number: AU-987654321
Effective Date: January 1, 2024 to December 31, 2024

I. COVERAGE DETAILS
1. Liability Coverage: This policy provides protection against legal liability resulting from bodily injury or property damage caused by an accident for which the insured is legally responsible. This includes coverage for third-party medical expenses and vehicular repairs.
2. Collision Coverage: Covers repairs or replacement of the insured vehicle if it is involved in a collision with another vehicle or object, regardless of fault.
3. Comprehensive Coverage: Provides coverage for damages to the insured vehicle caused by events other than a collision, including but not limited to theft, vandalism, fire, natural disasters, and falling objects.
4. Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage: Protects the insured if they are involved in an accident with a driver who does not have adequate insurance to cover the costs of bodily injury or property damage.
5. Personal Injury Protection (PIP): Covers medical expenses and, in some cases, lost wages and other damages regardless of who is at fault in an accident.

II. EXCLUSIONS
This policy does not cover:
1. Wear and Tear: Normal depreciation, wear and tear, or mechanical breakdown.
2. Intentional Damage: Any damage or injury intentionally caused by the insured.
3. Commercial Use: Use of the vehicle for commercial purposes, including ridesharing or delivery services, unless explicitly endorsed.
4. Unlicensed Drivers: Accidents occurring while the vehicle is operated by a person without a valid driver's license.
5. Racing: Damage occurring while participating in any prearranged or organized racing or speed contest.

III. CLAIM LIMITS
1. Bodily Injury Liability: Limited to $100,000 per person and $300,000 per accident.
2. Property Damage Liability: Limited to $50,000 per accident.
3. Collision and Comprehensive: Limited to the actual cash value (ACV) of the vehicle at the time of loss, not exceeding the cost to repair or replace.
4. Medical Payments: Limited to $5,000 per person.

IV. DEDUCTIBLE INFORMATION
1. Collision Deductible: A $500 deductible applies to all collision claims.
2. Comprehensive Deductible: A $250 deductible applies to all comprehensive claims.
3. Liability: No deductible applies to liability claims.
The insured is responsible for paying the deductible amount out of pocket before the insurance coverage begins to cover the remaining costs of the claim.
